This may be the best motherboard for my purposes I've scene yet.

make:        Supermicro
model:       a1sri-2758f
form factor: mini-ITX

price: $349.99 + $5.49 shipping (amazon Sun Aug 4, 2019)


Features
========
cpu:  Intel Atom Processor C2758 (Apollo Lake series)
      8-core, speed ???, 20W TDP

ram:   64GB max, Unbuffered ECC SODIMM, DDR3-1600MHz, in 4 DIMM slots
pcie:  1x PCI-E 2.0 x8
sata:  4x SATA2 (3 Gbps) ports; 2 SATA3 (6 Gbps)
       PCI-E sata card needed
lan:   Quad LAN with Intel® C2000 SoC I354

ipmi:  IPMI2.0, NMI, SuperDoctor® 5, Watchdog

i/o back panel:
  A. COM1             G. LAN1
  B. USB Port 2.0-0   H. LAN3
  C. USB Port 2.0-1   I. LAN2
  D. IPMI LAN         J. LAN4
  E. USB Port 3.0-0   K. VGA
  F. USB Port 3.0-1

  Wow, no hdmi only vga!
  4x GigE lan + IPMI wow!

power: 4-pin 12v DC power connector, ACPI power management,
       ATX Power connector, Control of power-on for recovery
       from AC power loss, C
